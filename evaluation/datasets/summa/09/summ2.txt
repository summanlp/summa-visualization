The World Cup has become the biggest social event in Web history.
The final game alone spurred 280 million interactions by 88 million people on Facebook, and there were a total of 32 million tweets.
In only a month, 350 million Facebok users generated 3 billion World Cup posts, comments and likes.
More than 1 billion people engaged with World Cup content through FIFA's website, social media accounts and mobile app.
The online attention were bolstered by growing interest in the United States, whose fans' interest didn't disappear when their team lost the cup.
