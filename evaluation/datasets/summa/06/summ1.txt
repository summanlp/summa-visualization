Standarized test are for measuring the level of education students have achieved.
Given that budgets depends on the result of these tests, teachers teachs students to take test, when they should be teaching them to think.
Tests only check if the answer is right, but they don't take into account the way students arrive to the answer nor the critical thinking behind that.
As a consequence of this technique, when college require students to analyze the main facts and draw conclusions, they aren't able to do it, revealing the failure of the aproach.
