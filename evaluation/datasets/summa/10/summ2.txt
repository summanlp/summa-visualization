Argentine comedy Wild Tales (Relatos Salvajes) received an Oscar nomination for best foreign-language film, the seventh in Argentina's history.
The film has received several international accolades, including a nomination for the Palme d'Or at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival, where it was a hit.
Szifron's film has become the most-viewed domestic film of all time in Argentina, with more than 3 million viewers.
The cast includes Ricardo Darin, who starred in The Secret in Their Eyes, an Argentine film that won the 2010 Oscar for best foreign-language film.
Also, argentine screenwriters Armando Bo and Nicolas Giacobone were nominated for Best Original Screenplay in the movie Birdman.
