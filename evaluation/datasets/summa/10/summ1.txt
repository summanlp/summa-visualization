The argentinian movie 'Wild Tales' has achieved an Oscar nomination for best foreign-language film.
Wild Tales, a comedy about violence, includes six independent stories about people who lose control in stressful situations.
Praised for its narration and sense of humour, the film has received several international accolades, specially in 2014 Cannes Film Festival.
Also, the argentinians screenwriters Armando Bo and Nicolas Giacobone were nominated for Best Original Screenplay for the movie Birdman.
