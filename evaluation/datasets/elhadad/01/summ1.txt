The key to treating cancer is to catch it early . But identifying the subtle changes in cells that betray their turncoat tendencies requires skill - and good luck - on the part of pathologists .
Matritech , a Massachusetts - based start - up company , has developed a diagnostic technique that detects bladder cancer more easily - and possibly more effectively - than existing methods can . Matritech 's test , which the company expects the Food and Drug Administration to approve by this summer , measures the amount of a particular type of protein in urine .
